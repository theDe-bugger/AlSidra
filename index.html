Business Exellence

 

Sustainability


At Al Sidra, we believe that the scarcity of natural resources must be transformed from a constraint to an opportunity. Our vision is to lead this transformation, being the reference in building sustainability services. Therefore, We acknowledge our responsibility to the planet and are continuously seeking new ways to reduce the environmental impact of our operations. ‘Sustainability’ for us, is a best practice that is embedded into every aspect of our business.

A full asset evaluation is conducted as part of all our contract mobilizations to identify the opportunities where we can implement energy and cost savings in our portfolio. We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint, which in turn produces triple bottom line benefits. Some of our sustainable practices include;

  • Resource Efficiency Management 
  • Base-lining, utility consumption tracking Vs target and reporting
  • Green procurement practice
  • Implementation of green office guidelines
  • Waste reduction & recycling

Certifications

Our Integrated Management System (IMS) has been certified to ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 standards. We are committed to continuously improve our integrated management system, so as to enhance customer satisfaction, quality of service, safety and welfare of our people and environment.

In addition, we have successfully implemented ISO 22000 to safeguard our food. The Health, Safety and Environment Management System (HSEMS) is the reference by which Al Sidra M E  ensures compliance with applicable HSE rules and legislations. Our HSE management system is approved by:

  • Occupational Safety & Health Centre of Abu
    Dhabi (OSHAD)
  • Abu Dhabi Agriculture and Food Safety Authority
    (ADAFSA)
